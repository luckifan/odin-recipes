Description

Beans wrapped in a flour tortilla

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Flour tortilla
  • Dried corn tortillas
  • canola oil

Steps

  1. Heat a small amount of oil in pot and fry any amount of dried tortillas to give the oil a nice flavor
  2. Pour beans into pot and let them bubble a little, stir so as to not let the beans burn
  3. mash beans once you've let them heat up
  4. Heat flour tortilla on stove
  5. take tortilla off the stove once it has been sufficiently warmed up
  6. Add beans into the middle off the torilla
  7. Wrap it.
