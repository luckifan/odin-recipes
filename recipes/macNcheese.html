Mac N Cheese

Home

Description

Kraft Mac N Cheese is an awful food for you but I love eating it.

Ingredients

  • Kraft Mac N Cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. bring pot of water to a boil
  2. add noodles
  3. let noodles soften
  4. once noodles are to desired softness, turn off stove, drain water, return to stove with heat off
  5. add cheese packet, 4tbs of butter, and milk (however much you want)
  6. mix
  7. serve.
